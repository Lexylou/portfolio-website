About

Hey there, Alexa here!

I am a self taught web designer, developer and front-end engineer, and I discovered my love for coding and front and development in late 2020.

I graduated from Washington State University (Go Cougs!) in May of 2019 earning my Bachelor of Science in Bioengineering with minors in Entrepreneurship and Mathematics. Shortly after, I held a position as a Field Account Executive for an occupational health company. I learned a ton through this experience, but I decided that I wanted to further grow in a profession where I could utilize my strong problem-solving skills along-side my creativity, and through this, I discovered my passion for front-end engineering and web development!

I am mainly self-taught with the assistance of an online coding platform, Codecademy, and I have learned, mastered and continue to build the skills on the many aspects that make up this exciting field. With all the new skills I am developing (no pun intended, really), I can't wait to become a front-end developer!
